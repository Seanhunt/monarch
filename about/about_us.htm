Monarch Stables offers a unique variety of youth and amateur oriented programs. Our goal is to maximize each rider's individual potential and assist them in achieving their personal goals. Monarch Stables is dedicated to the development of successful horsemanship skills both in and out of the show ring. Lessons are available to riders of all levels.

After her junior exhibitor show career ended, Brigitte pursued her educational degree at Cal Poly Pomona University. She received her BA in Liberal Arts with a minor in Equine Science. She went on to obtain her California State teaching credential and spent 10 years teaching elementary, then junior high school in the Los Angeles area (Brigitte still retains a California State Teaching Credential).
 
 
Brigitte's experience over the years has included many different breeds and disciplines with her primary emphasis remaining on the American Saddlebred. She has worked privately with two breeding ranches (Andalusian and Saddlebred) where she started and developed many young horses that are successful in and out of the show ring.

Brigitte Scholl has been involved with Saddlebreds for over 25 years. Her early years were spent learning the art of equitation and ringmanship. Continuous weekend tournaments, parades and horse shows taught Brigitte, at an early age the craft of preparing and turning out a horse for performances. It was not long until Brigitte found herself in charge of all young riders and horses in the barn, a job she took great pride in performing.
During these years Brigitte had the opportunity to catch ride for many top barns on the west coast as well as work several young horses in her spare time. Her catch riding produced many Championships and National Championships with Andalusians, National Show Horses and Saddlebreds.
In 1999, Brigitte combined her two great talents, horses and teaching when she opened Monarch Stables. Under this banner, she has trained some of the top amateur and junior exhibitor teams on the west coast. Monarch Stables, originally located in Malibu has found the perfect home at a beautiful facility located in Santa Rosa, California. Brigitte is a USEF licensed official in the Saddlebred and Saddle Seat Equitation divisions.
