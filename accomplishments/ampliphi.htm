CH Ampli-Phi & Stephanie Duck swept the Junior Exhibitor Three Gaited Show Pleasure Championship and qualifier at the Northwest Fall Classic Horse Show in Salem, OR. Stephanie Duck catch rode for owner Julie Streitfeld, and teamed the charismatic Ampli-Phi beautifully. It was not only Stephanie's first time out with Danny, but also Danny's debut with a junior exhibitor. Both suited each other well and show their gameness and poise by turning in two flawless performances.

Congratulations Stephanie and Danny, we love you both!

 
Chapter One Champion
CH Ampli-Phi earned his championship designation in 2003 at the UPHA Chapter One Horse Show and returned in 2004 to remain on top. CH Ampli-Phi was named Park Pleasure Champion and Reserve Grand Champion.

"Danny" has also shown his diversity in harness where he claimed pleasure driving championships and reserves at Menlo Circus Club, Monterey and Santa Rosa earlier in the season.
 
Grand Ending to a Champion Style Season!
CH Ampli-Phi was recently named the Park Pleasure Grand Champion at the Arizona Futurity Horse Show. One class after CH Ampli-Phi's victory, team mate Su Casa was named five gaited show pleasure Grand Champion. These champions are owned, exhibited and loved by Julie Streitfeld.
CH Ampli-Phi demonstrated his consistency throughout the season and was awarded the NCASHA 2003 High Point Champion for the Park Division.
CH Ampli-Phi, better known as "Danny" has earned a well deserved winter vacation and is cutting it up in the pasture with friends.
 
 
Ampli-Phi Receives Lifetime Championship Designation!
It has been another great season for Ampli-Phi. This sparkling Show Pleasure star has made a splash with owner Julie Streitfeld the past three seasons. Danny, as he is affectionately known, and Julie have won at every major show on the West Coast and received many top honors and year-end awards, including being named 2001 ASHA California State Pleasure Champion.
Trainer Brigitte Scholl has kept this consistent team the one to beat in the highly competitive pleasure field.
Always at the top of his division, Danny stepped up to Park Pleasure this year to continue his winning ways with Brigitte Scholl. This team exhibited impressive victories at the Monterey Springfest and UPHA Chapter 1 and earned Danny's final points need for CHampionship designation.
Friends, fans and family of Monarch Stables and Ampli-Phi gathered to celebrate with the recently named CHampion at the picturesque Santa Barbara National Horse Show.
Congratulations to CH AMPLI-PHI for receiving this prestigious recognition of your accomplishments!!
