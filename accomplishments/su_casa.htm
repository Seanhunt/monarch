Racking On
Su Casa continues to be the leader of his gaited division in 2004. "Casa Man" never backed an ear as he racked up a storm and was named Five Gaited Country Pleasure Champion and Reserve Grand Champion at the Santa Barbara National.

Watch for Su Casa next at the NWSA Fall Classic.
 
Two for Two
Su Casa and Julie Streitfeld have started the 2004 season out strong with two shows and two Grand Championship performances. Watch for this exciting team next at Del Mar Charity Fair and Santa Barbara National.
 
End of the Season Marks a Great Beginning
New Combination Su Casa and Julie Streitfeld headed to the Arizona Futurity Horse Show after only one month together. This dynamic team gave two exciting performances and were named Champion and Grand Champion in the five gaited show pleasure division.

The tricolor, garland and Dave Coats Memorial Perpetual Trophy were presented to Su Casa in the Stake on Saturday evening.

Watch for them in 2004!
