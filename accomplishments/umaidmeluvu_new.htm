Great Season Equals High Point Honor
UmaidmeLuvu and Stephanie Duck shared a fantastic 2003 show season together. This Beautiful pair claimed wins at Monterey Springfest, UPHA Chapter 1, and Del Mar Charity Fair Horse Show.

UmaidmeLuvu and Stephanie were honored at the recent NCASHA awards banquet and were named 2003 Three Gaited Show Pleasure High Point Champions.

Congratulations!
 
A Great Start for a Young New Team
As a four year old, Umaidmeluvu was started and developed by trainer Brigitte Scholl. Stephanie Duck and her family purchased this young mare by the famous sire, Periaptor, late last year.

Stephanie and her elegant bay, Umaidmeluvu, set out to make their mark in 2003. This being Stephanie's first show season showing at the "A" rated circuit, they had much to accomplish. Over the winter, the team had worked hard and attended several schooling shows to make sure they were ready, and ready they were!

The first show of the season they were Champion and Grand Champion in their pleasure classes and Reserve Champion in equitation. Since then, they have been named Champion or Reserve Champion at every show they have attended and it doesn't seem to be slowing down.

This relatively young team has stood toe to toe with the most experienced competitors this season and have shown how much potential they possess.
To watch these two in the show ring many are struck with their brightness and quiet confidence that is far beyond their years. We expect great things from this perfectly matched team. They have their sights set high and off to a great start.

Watch for them next at The Arizona Futurity Horse Show!!
