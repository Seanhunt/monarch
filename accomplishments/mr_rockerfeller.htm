Perfect Season Finale'
Mr. Rockefeller and Alexandra Johnson headed to the Northwest Fall Classic after having success all season in the Junior Exhibitor Three Gaited Division in 2004. "Alex and Nick" turned in two exciting performances and were named Three Gaited Amateur Champion and Grand Champion.

Congratulations Alex, we are so proud of you!
 
Beautiful Combination Proves Successful Once Again!
Alexandra Johnson and the handsome Mr. Rockefeller proved successful once again, this time taking the Arizona Horse Show by storm. They hit the show ring in style and this beautiful team captured the Junior Exhibitor Three Gaited qualifier and Grand Championship. For their efforts, Alex and "Nick" were presented with an impressive perpetual trophy, garland and tricolor.
Alexandra Johnson and Mr. Rockefeller next showed their poise in the Equitation Division. After a workout of the top riders, they were named Reserve Grand Champions in the Saddle Seat Equitation Championship.
Look forward to 2004!
 
Champions First Time Out!
Double congratulations are in order for rider Alexandra Johnson. First, for her recent selection of the handsome gelding, Mr. Rockefeller, and second for being named Equitation Champion and Grand Champion at the NCASHA Fall Show the first time out with her new horse.
Alex and "Nick" looked the part as they exited the ring with the tri-color ribbon against highly competitive and more seasoned teams. This well suited team has started off right and will certainly prove to be top contenders in the coming months.
This beautiful young lady and rider is proudly encouraged by her parents, Charmagne Bagley and Robbie Johnson.
Watch for them at the Arizona Futurity Horse Show!
