Three for Three in 2006
 
 
 
NWSA Fall Classic and Futurity
Siri is accompanied by her parents Chris and Donna Hadland after her Championship ride.
 
Siri Hadland and CH Blue Ridge Miles executed their rail and pattern work beautifully as they claimed the North West Fall Classic Saddle Seat Equitation Championship on Saturday night. Earlier in the week Siri and Miles turned in two other elegant performances to earn the blue in Saddle Seat Equitation 14-17 and reserve in the USEF Saddle Seat Medal.

Congratulations to Siri and Miles for a stellar 2005 season!
  • 2005 Pacific Coast Horse Shows Circuit Saddle Seat Equitation 14-17 High Point Champion
  • 2005 NCASHA Saddle Seat Equitation 14-17 High Point Champion
 
Santa Barbara National 2005
 
UPHA Chapter 1 2005
 
The UPHA Chapter 1 Horse Show brought out the best equitation riders on the West Coast. Siri Hadland and CH Blue Ridge Miles made a strong first impression and were named UPHA Challenge Cup Reserve Champions. This elegant team gained momentum as the show continued and performed flawlessly, rising to the top of the Saddle Seat Equitation 14-17.
 
Monterey Springfest 2005
Siri Hadland and her beautiful mount, CH Blue Ridge Miles turned in several top performances at the Monterey Springfest Horse Show. Together, Siri and Miles earned the blue in the Saddle Seat Equation for riders 14-17 and then went on to be named the Equation Reserve Grand Champion.

Great job Siri and Miles!
 
Siri Hadland and CH Blue Ridge Miles started their 2005 season off right at the Menlo Circus Club Tournament. They looked well polished and earned themselves the blue in the Saddle Seat Equitation 14-17 qualifier and Saddle Seat Equitation Championship.

Siri and Miles capped last season at the Northwest Fall Classic at Salem, Oregon with a beautiful performance to win the UPHA Challenge Cup Class. CH Blue Ridge Miles also stepped up to the Park division, with trainer Brigitte Scholl and topped a beautiful class, NWSA Park Horse Open.

Siri Hadland was also honored in 2004 by NCASHA where she was named the Saddle Seat Equitation 14-17 High Point Champion.
 
2004 Barbara and Sonny Cannon Scholarship
Friday evening at the Santa Barbara National Horse Show, Siri Hadland was formally presented as the 2004 Barbara and Sonny Cannon Scholarship Winner by the Pacific Saddlebred Association. Siri and her handsome mount, CH Blue Ridge Miles, were accompanied in center ring by parents, Donna and Chris Hadland; trainer, Brigitte Scholl and PSA Scholarship committee members.

Congratulations to Siri Hadland and thank you to the Pacific Saddlebred Association.
 
USAEF Medal Champions
Siri Hadland and CH Blue Ridge Miles teamed perfectly to claim the USAEF Saddle Seat Medal Championship at Del Mar Charity Fair Horse Show. Siri and her new mount, the well known park horse CH Blue Ridge Miles, executed a beautiful pattern which took them to the top of a nice class.

Look for Siri and Miles to continue to shine in and out of the show ring in upcoming months.
 
Scholarship Honor
Siri Hadland has been selected to receive the 2004 Barbara and Sonny Cannon Memorial Scholarship by the Pacific Saddlebred Association. This scholarship comes at a special time for Siri as she just purchased a new horse, the beautiful Park Champion, CH Blue Ridge Miles. After an extensive search, The Hadland Family selected "Miles" to become Siri's partner in the Equitation Division for 2004.

This new combination of horse and rider fit together perfectly and are making a big impression on the West Coast. Watch for them at Del Mar and Santa Barbara!
