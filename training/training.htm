Training & Lessons
Monarch Stables is a full service training facility. Our specialty is teaming horse and rider combinations and preparing them for the show ring. Great care is taken to match horses that compliment and challenge each rider. We strive to bring each of these teams to a higher level of communication and performance. Whether you are pleasure riding or are headed to the show ring, Monarch Stables can help you to reach you personal goals.
In our show programs, horses are available for lease or purchase. We attend most West Coast competitions and many schooling shows throughout the year.

Trainer Brigitte Scholl is passionate about working with young horses. She takes great pride in her proven ability to develop young stock.
Brigitte’s patience and consistency in her training methods foster confidence in young horses so that they are better able to reach their full potential. Monarch Stables offers a nice selection of young prospects at all times.
 
Lessons
Monarch Stables offers beginning through advanced riding lessons. Riding instructor, Lynn Silva, holds many years of teaching and showing experience. Using her patience and skill, she develops beginning riders into future show ring stars. The program emphasizes safety, horsemanship, and good sportsmanship above all.

Riders with horses in show training take instruction with Brigitte Scholl to prepare them for show ring competition.
